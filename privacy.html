1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

We collect and store information you provide to us or that we gather when you use the App. This includes:

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and any optional details you choose to provide.
  • Public Content: Any notes, reviews, or photos you post within the App may be visible to other users.
  • Device Information: We collect information such as your IP address, device type, operating system, and browser type to improve our services.
  • Location Data: If you allow location access, we may collect location data to suggest nearby restaurants. You can manage this in your device settings.
  • Communications: If you contact us for support or inquiries, we may store your messages for future reference.
  • Transactional Information: If you make purchases or subscriptions through the App, we collect payment details necessary to process your transactions.

We use this information to enhance the App’s functionality, personalize your experience, provide customer support, ensure security, and comply with legal obligations.

2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve user experience and analyze trends. These may include:

  • Necessary Cookies: Enable essential functionality like login sessions.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the App.
  • Advertising Cookies: Allow us to show relevant ads based on your preferences.

You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We may share your information with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors who assist with hosting, analytics, payment processing, and customer support.
  • Advertising Partners: To display personalized ads within the App.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose user data to regulatory authorities.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

4. Controlling Your Personal Data

You can:

  • Modify your account settings to update or delete personal information.
  • Manage privacy settings to control visibility of your notes and reviews.
  • Opt-out of marketing emails by following the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

5. Data Retention and Account Termination

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will remove your personal data except where retention is required for legal reasons or legitimate business purposes.

6. Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under this age. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at foodiefolder@cxcs.us.

7. Security Measures

8. Dispute Resolution

9. Jurisdiction and International Data Transfers
